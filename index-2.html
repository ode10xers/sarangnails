]]> As the New Year approaches, it’s time to start thinking about the perfect nail designs to match the festivities. Whether you’re looking for something subtle or something bold, there are plenty of options to choose from. From sparkly glitter to intricate designs, here are some of the top New Years nail designs to try out this year.

Glitter is always a great option for New Years nails. Whether you opt for a subtle glitter ombre or a full-on glitter explosion, it’s sure to make your nails stand out. If you want to keep it classic, you can always go for a classic red and gold combination. For a more modern look, try a combination of silver and black. You can also add a bit of sparkle with some rhinestones or sequins.

No matter what look you’re going for, you can’t go wrong with a classic French manicure. It’s a timeless look that never goes out of style. For a more festive look, try adding some gold or silver glitter to the tips. If you’re feeling creative, you can even try out a New Years-themed design. Try adding some stars, fireworks, or other festive elements to your nails.
